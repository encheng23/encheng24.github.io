Advance the health of the public
Enhance understanding about the determinants of cancer and other chronic diseases

Hi, I'm En Cheng (MD, MSPH), the first-year PhD student of Department of Chronic Disease Epidemiology at Yale University. My research interest is cancer epidemiology (particularly in GI cancer), covering etiology, prevention, treatment, survival, and complications.

Before coming to Yale, I received my Medical Degree from Peking University, and Master of Science in Public Health (Epidemiology) from Emory.

I also practice karate in my spare time. Currently I'm a member of Yale Shotokan Karate with the brown belt YSK Pages
