Advance the health of the public
Enhance understanding about the determinants of cancer and other chronic diseases

Hi, I'm En Cheng (MD, PhD), a Research Postdoctoral Fellow at Kaiser Permanente Division of Research starting from June 2021. My primary mentor is Dr. Elizabeth M. Cespedes Feliciano (Research Scientist II). My research interest is cancer epidemiology (particularly in gastrointestinal cancer and breast cancer), covering etiology, prevention, treatment, survival, and complications.

I received my Doctor of Philosophy (Epidemiology) from Yale University, Master of Science in Public Health (Epidemiology) from Emory University, and Medical Degree from Peking University.

I also practice karate in my spare time and am a black belt (Shodan). Currently I'm a member of UCSF Mission Bay Dojo at JKA of California, and used to train with Yale Shotokan Karate and Emory Shotokan Karate Club.
